Introduction to Spiritual knowledge

  • Course Information

    • This course is for professionals who are looking for a way to introduce the idea of spirituality into their working life. To give the individuals within the organisation a chance to remove old ideas of what the word spirituality means to them and to find a new definition of themselves within the organisation that they are a part.
    Using the ancient knowledge embedded in spiritual teaching to teach people the importance of working together for the good of the company.
    These fourteen days, are split up into different lectures and workshops to teach the importance of understanding our higher consciousness, our connection with the oneness of the universe and will cover the importance of meditation in it's three main forms.
