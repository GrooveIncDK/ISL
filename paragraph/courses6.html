Building self-esteem

  • Course Information

    • Building self-esteem is a life long process, we confront our selves every day. Each choice we make can bring to us more joy and happiness or destroy our own feeling of self worth.
    This course will enable you to rebuild the foundation on which to rebuild your life. It will not give you back your life, but it will give you the tools to build a new life, from the ground up.
