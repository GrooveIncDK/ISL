Introduction to Spirituality

  • Course Information

    • This course has been designed as a way to introduce the student to the idea of spirituality, To give the individual a chance to remove old ideas of what the word spirituality means and to find a new definition of themselves through the ancient knowledge embedded in spiritual teaching.
    Within these fourteen days, you will learn about your own higher consciousness, your connection with the oneness of the universe and you will learn about meditation in it's three main forms.
