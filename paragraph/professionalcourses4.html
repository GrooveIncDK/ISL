  • Understanding your customer

    • This course is an adaptation of the course of miracles, and has been tailor made for business executives and people that need a better understanding of how to communicate with their customers.
    The purpose is to train your mind in a systematic way to a different perception of everyone and everything in the world. So that you will understand that each experience is equally applicable to everyone and everything you experience.
    The overall aim is to increase your ability to extend the ideas you will be practising to include everything.
    The exercises demand that new conditions necessary for this kind of transfer.
