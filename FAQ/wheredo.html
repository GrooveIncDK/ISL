Where do I start?

You have already begun the process of change by finding the Institute of Spiritual Learning.
Congratulations

Some people like the soft approach by buying a book and reading through the material.
In our experience the only way to change is to challenge yourself to change.

This is where ISL comes in, read through the website and get a feel for what it is you are looking for.
Then you can either order the on-line course you like the sound of or contact someone from the
institute to set up a meeting to discuss your own personal needs in more detail.
This can be done over the phone, via e-mail or in person.
This is free and is without obligation.

If you know that what you are looking for requires one to one coaching.
Then you can call the booking and information number for more details and
someone will be more than happy to guide you through the process.
