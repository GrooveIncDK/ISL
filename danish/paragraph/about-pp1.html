The advantage of lieing to yourself

What a wonderful universe we live in, where people can continue to lie and deceive themselves every day.

  • I am so happy with my job.
  • My life is just what I wanted it to be.
  • How lucky I am that I have this shiny new car.

    • The greatest thing that the universe ever did was give you what you want.
    If you want to lie to yourself and you want to live an unhappy life, then this wonderful and fantastic universe in all it's majestic brilliance will give you just what you want.
