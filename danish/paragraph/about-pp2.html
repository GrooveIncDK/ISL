Being afraid of yourself

We are all afraid of something, we are all running away from the one thing about ourselves that we don't like.
Being afraid of yourself is a good thing, if it wasn't for fear you wouldn't know there was anything you needed to change. The key to getting to know yourself better is to accept what it is that you are afraid of, and just accept it as a warning to yourself that you have gone too far.
Yes that Tiger is dangerous take a step back.
