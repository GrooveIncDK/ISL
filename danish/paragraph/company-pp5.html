Communication

This lecture highlights the differences between masculine and feminine energies, and the influence it has on the way we communicate.
Why are we different and how can we turn it to our advantage.
What that communication is?
What happens with our emotions when we speak with other people?
How much do we communicate without actually opening our mouths.
How do we handle conflict and how can we avoid being affected by other people's emotions as we communicate.

These and many other questions will be discussed, including a brief outline of the pseudo phenomenological theories of Dr. R.D. Lang and Professor C.G. Jung.
