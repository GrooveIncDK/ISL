WHat is a Spiritual Guide?

Benjamin James Matthew
Teacher & Spritual Guide


A spiritual guide is a teacher of the inner wisdom.
A spiritual guide is a student of and a disciple of God.
A spiritual guide has the ability to see past themselves.
A spiritual guide is a person who has travelled the path you are on.
A spiritual guide has an understanding of the need for people to define themselves.
A spiritual guide is an aspirant to the truth and faith of the oneness of consciousness.
A spiritual guide is a person who has climbed further up the mountain of consciousness.
A spiritual guide has a retrospective outlook on our present socio and political perception of the world.

And above all,
A spiritual guide has belief in the greatness and power of the incomprehensible force that loves, governs and guides every living thing in the whole universe.
